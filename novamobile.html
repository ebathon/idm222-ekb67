Fashion Nova Mobile Flow Redesign
January - March 2022

Overview

I analyzed the mobile design for the Fashion Nova, specifically the user flow for finding a pair of jeans. I rebranded and redesigned the flow, making a more streamlined and visually appealing process.

The Challenge

Fashion Nova's flow was clutterrd and lacked organization. To improve it, I needed to create clear filters and add intuitive ways for people to see and find what style of jeans they are looking for.

Pain Points

  • Confusing Web Layout
  • Lack of navigation for item specificity
  • Necessary information is not prominent
